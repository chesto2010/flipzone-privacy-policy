Flipzone – Support

Thank you for playing Flipzone! If you need help or have a question, we're happy to assist.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the game?
Tap anywhere on the screen to flip gravity. Time your taps to navigate through the obstacles and survive as long as possible.

How does the leaderboard work?
Flipzone uses Apple Game Center for global leaderboards. Make sure you are signed into Game Center in your iPhone settings to participate.

How do I sign into Game Center?
Go to Settings → Game Center on your iPhone and sign in with your Apple ID.

The leaderboard is not showing my score. What should I do?
Make sure you have an active internet connection and are signed into Game Center. Your score will sync automatically after your next game.

How do I get the Score Boost?
After a game over, you can watch a short rewarded video ad to activate a Score Boost for your next run.
