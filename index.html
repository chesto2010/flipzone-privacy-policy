Privacy Policy – Flipzone

Last updated: March 2026

Flipzone ("the Game") is developed by Christoph Mayer ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the Game and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

Flipzone itself does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. Your high score is stored locally on your device only.

However, the Game integrates third-party services that may collect data:

2. Apple Game Center

Flipzone uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. When you participate in leaderboards, your Game Center display name and score are submitted to Apple. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

3. Unity Ads

Flipzone uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect the following data to serve and measure ads:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

You can limit ad tracking in your iPhone settings under Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Unity's Privacy Policy is available at: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Children's Privacy

Flipzone is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
